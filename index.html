South Sudan: History, Way of Living, Conflicts, and Peace

South Sudan, the world’s youngest country, gained independence in 2011 after decades of conflict with Sudan. The region’s diverse ethnic groups have lived there since the 15th century, enduring colonial rule and struggles for autonomy. How has South Sudan’s complex history shaped its national identity?
